Why I hate VALORANT

By: 3xTomorrow

VALORANT is such an interesting game.. but it comes with consequences if you play it for an extremely long amount of time! At first, you enjoy it. You enjoy the game, the characters, the abilities, the guns, the skins, the maps, the game modes, and everything else. But then, you see the true side of VALORANT. The dark side.

You start playing comp...

Now you must think. Isn't this a good thing? You say to yourself, "I want to improve at the game so I should do something challenging like playing competitive." And at first it works. You go from Iron 2, to Bronze 3. Then it starts to go down the hill. You get to silver. Oh boy...

A smurf shows up in your game. You might be thinking that this smurf will carry you. You are so wrong. He is going to throw your game. He is going to take the spike and shove it up the enemy's side so far that your team will get picked off one by one if someone tries to get it. Your team doesn't pick the right team-comps. There are five duelists on the team and nobody is rushing in on site and getting the bomb down.

Of course eventually, you'll get out of these lobbies like I did. Eventually everything will be okay if you get into a high-elo lobby like mine right? Everyone is high-IQ, they know how to play the game and make plays. God you are overestimating high-elo. It's just as bad as low-elo. You might get better aiming teammates, better team-comps, and so on. But the community is not as good. The people are toxic, people still throw your games because they don't care anymore, and the communication. The communication is so awful. Nobody talks. Nobody makes callouts. Nobody does anything. You are left to fend for yourself. It is a 1v5 every round. And when you miss a shot, guess who gets blamed? Nobody but you.

I have been playing VALORANT for a long time now. I've been playing ever since it released. Episode 1, Act 1. This game has changed. But the community and the people that play it have been the same ever since the beginning. I have been in Iron, Bronze, Silver, Gold, Plat, and Diamond. I have seen it all. I have seen the throwers and the smurfs. I have won and I have lost and suffered.

I hate VALORANT. And if you ever think of playing it, prepare for the worst. Trust me, you will need that good mentality.
